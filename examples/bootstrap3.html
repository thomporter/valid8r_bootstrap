Valid8r for Twitter Bootstrap3

Standard Form

Standard Forms look like this (in jade:)

form(role="form")
  .form-group
    label.control-label(for="test1") Test 1
    input.form-control(type="text", name="test1", id="test1")

Notice the control-label class on the label. Without it, the label will not turn red on error.

Horizontal Form

Horizontal Forms look like this (in jade:)

form.form-horizontal(role="form")
.form-group
  label.col-sm-2.control-label(for="test1") Test 1
  .col-sm-10
    input.form-control(type="text", name="test1", id="test1")
